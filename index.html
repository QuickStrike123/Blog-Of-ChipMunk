places must visit

prithivi cafe

i had visited 2018

Quaint little restraount tucked inside a bylane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrence to the cafe. so be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Priviti cafe" while main road. the restraunt has a resanable good menu at resanable prices. the food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. be sure to book in advance to acoid dissapointments. on other days,the crowd there is pretty hep & happining. hosts the famouse " prithivi theater feast ", which you must attend if possible.

jantar mantatar - jaipur

i had visited in feb 2019

it is a wonderful place. must visit this place. must hired tour guide so it will be better to understand things. it is a beutiful maintained small complex with yellow painted walls. it's a historical site famous among tourists. it contains a number of diffferent devices that were used to measure time in different eras in the indian history. it has a normal price of 15 for indians but above 225-250 for forginers. nice place to visit. we had a incredible expirience visiting this observatory and seeing the accuracy of this astromical instruments built around 300 years ago.
